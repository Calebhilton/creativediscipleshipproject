Moroni 1

Moroni wanders alone after the destruction of his people and hides from the Lamanites and is determined to write a few more words for future generations.

Moroni 2

Shows Jesus Christ giving His disciples the power to confer the gift of the Holy Ghost by the laying on of hands after baptism.

Moroni 3

Describes how the disciples of Christ ordained priests and teachers by the laying on of hands, giving them authority to lead and teach the people.

Moroni 4

The exact words of the sacrament prayer on the bread, showing how priests bless and break the bread in remembrance of Jesus Christ's body.

Moroni 5

The exact words of the sacrament prayer on the wine (or water), showing how priests bless the cup in remembrance of the blood of Jesus Christ.

Moroni 6

Describes how people were baptized after showing true repentance, and how the church met often to pray, partake of the sacrament, and care for one another in love and righteousness.

Moroni 7

Teaches that faith leads us to believe in Christ, hope gives us confidence in eternal life, and charity is the pure love of Christ that we must have to become like Him.

Moroni 8

Mormon’s letter to Moroni, teaching that little children are innocent, baptism is for the repentant, and that the love and mercy of Jesus Christ saves all who come unto Him with humility.

Moroni 9

Mormon writing to Moroni about the terrible wickedness and violence among both the Nephites and Lamanites, urging him to have faith, hope in Christ, and be filled with charity despite the evil around him.

Moroni 10

Moroni’s final testimony, inviting all to ask God if the Book of Mormon is true, promising that by the power of the Holy Ghost, truth will be revealed, and urging all to come unto Christ through faith, hope, and charity.
