Ether 1

The story of the Jaredites, showing Jared and his brother praying for God’s help after the Tower of Babel is confounded, and beginning their journey of faith and obedience.

Ether 2

The brother of Jared leading his people to the seashore, where they wait for further direction from the Lord and are commanded to build barges to cross the ocean.

Ether 3

The brother of Jared’s great faith as he sees the finger of the Lord touch sixteen stones to give them light, and then sees the Lord Himself face to face.

Ether 4

The Lord commanding the brother of Jared to seal up his vision and writings until a future time, promising that those with great faith will receive greater revelations.

Ether 5

A message from Moroni to future readers, explaining that three witnesses will testify of the truth of the Book of Mormon and of the sacred records shown to them by God.

Ether 6

The Jaredites faithfully crossing the ocean in their barges, guided by the Lord with winds and lighted stones, until they safely reach the promised land.

Ether 7

Orihah becoming the first Jaredite king, leading in righteousness, but later generations fall into pride and conflict, resulting in wars among the people.

Ether 8

The rise of secret combinations among the Jaredites, beginning with Akish, whose wicked plans lead to murder, conspiracy, and the corruption of power.

Ether 9

The rise and fall of kings among the Jaredites, with both righteous and wicked rulers, as secret combinations and pride continue to bring destruction and suffering to the people.

Ether 10

A cycle of Jaredite kings, with both righteous rulers who bless the people with prosperity and wicked kings who lead them into war, pride, and secret combinations.

Ether 11

The Jaredite prophets warn the people to repent, but the people reject them, leading to more wars, secret combinations, and the gradual destruction of their civilization.

Ether 12

Teaches about the power and importance of faith, with Moroni recording Ether’s teachings and adding his own testimony of how faith leads to miracles, hope, and the witness of Christ.

Ether 13

The prophet Ether prophesies of the New Jerusalem and the future blessings for the righteous, while also witnessing the destruction and wickedness of his own people.

Ether 14

The Jaredite civilization collapes into chaos, as rival kings lead their people into terrible wars, widespread destruction, and the complete breakdown of their society.

Ether 15

The final, tragic battle of the Jaredites at the hill Ramah, where millions perish in war, and Coriantumr becomes the last surviving Jaredite king after witnessing the total destruction of his people.
